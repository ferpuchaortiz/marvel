Iron Man

Tony Stark is a billionaire inventor and founding member of the Avengers.

Character Overview

Iron Man first appeared in Marvel Comics in 1963. Created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby, Tony Stark built his first armored suit to escape captivity and later refined it to fight evil.

Powers & Abilities

  • Powered exoskeleton suit
  • Flight capability
  • Advanced weapon systems
  • Artificial intelligence integration
